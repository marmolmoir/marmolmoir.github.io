Shape Brain will consist of structured and shaped garments that serve as tangible juxtaposition to the soft curvature of the human body. Physically, we serve as a contrast against the rigid walls in our homes, the parallelism of the buildings in our cities. Extending that to fashion presents interesting silhouettes, I think.
