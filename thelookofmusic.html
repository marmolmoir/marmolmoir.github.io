the look of music

Oh, you found this! It's not done yet. :)

The Look of Music will consist of garments that are visualizations of how I perceive music. I've always thought of the concept as organized sound. Being classically trained for over a decade allows this to become innate. How my eyes see, how my brain thinks, and how my ears hear began to warp and shift.

This will serve as an interpretation of the intersection between fashion, music, and technology. Musicality is taught, then absorbed by every musician to bring out a sound that is unique to all ears. Musicality allows every musician to express their individuality by showcasing how they interpret music of all genres. I like to think that fashion follows that path.
