about

First jotted down on a notepad in 2021, marmolmoir serves as an umbrella for unisex clothing created by Filipino-American artist Mara de Guzman. Every garment is rooted in symmetry, botany, and usage of both culturally traditional and biomaterial-based textiles that are regenerative in nature.

Education

I am currently a 4th-year student at San Francisco State University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Apparel Design and Merchandising (concentration in Merchandising) and a minor in Marketing.
